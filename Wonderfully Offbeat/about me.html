I came bounding in the world during the year 1997 and still currently living and loving life. I don’t really care what others would say about me on how I look or on how I treat them. I don’t really like crowded places or even very noisy people, I prefer to spend my days reading, drinking coffee, and talking to people in the virtual world of internet.
